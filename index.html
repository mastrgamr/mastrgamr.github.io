uBooks

It's hard enough on the pockets for a student to buy the textbook they need for class every semester, and just as hard to get rid of it in the end. This app was created to help students get rid of their dead weight. Student ask peers for needed supplies instead of going online and waiting for the mailman. A marketplace created by students, for students.

Transit Pulse (BETA)

As a native New York Citian (that's a term right? Run with it), I take calculated steps towards getting to where I need to go on time. The MTA is back breaking with its service changes, re-routes, and that bus that likes to catch every red light. This app utilizes real-time data provided by the MTA in order to get you where you need to go, no surprises.
